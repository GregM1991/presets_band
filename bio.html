    • Kim Moyes

      Kimberley Isaac Moyes is an Australian musician, producer, DJ and one half of the Sydney-based electronica duo, The Presets (with Julian Hamilton). [1] Moyes provides synthesizers and drums, as well as engineering, production and songwriting.
